CD booklet

Inkscape helped me create the front and rear pages for the booklet of the Fantomized.info community compilation CD. This is the rear/front pages of a A5 booklet. There was nothing tricky with it - we printed A4 page and then wrapped it in the middle so we got a A5 booklet.

Book diagrams

I'm writing a book on sound design and synthesizer programming, and it requires lots of sound structure drawings and other sorts of graphics. Currently, my book has over 40 separate images, and all of them were created with Inkscape. Thanks to it's simplicity and intuitiveness, drawing these graphics is a real pleasure. I never thought someday I'll be able to create such nice graphics this easily. Amazing!

Logos

The main thing I love about Inkscape is that besides being a really feature-rich editor, it is also very simple and converient to use—and, most importantly, it inspired me to draw. These logos were created in minutes—and all thanks to Inkscape's intuitive interface and invaluable tools.
